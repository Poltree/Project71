  • Virtual Student Registration

    Due to COVID-19 stay-at-home orders and social distancing protocol, new student registration will temporarily be done virtually. Registration can be started by emailing the school(s) in which your student(s) will attend. Parents or guardians must submit the information below in addition to transcripts from any former schools. Children are eligible to attend kindergarten if they have reached the age of five years on or before September 1. Items Needed for Registration (see Regulation 5001 Enrollment and Registration Requirements and Verification of Residency for details) Proof of student's age Health and immunization record Two proofs of residency Before entering school in September, your child must have a physical examination.
